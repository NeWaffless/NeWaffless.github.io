A little more about me...

My name is Aidan Walbran and I’m a Sydney based programmer with a Bachelor in Design Computing from the University of Sydney. My skills and interests primarily revolve around games programming, though I also have worked with virtual reality development, web development, as well as visual and product design. I love tackling programming challenges as the end result is either developing a logical solution to the problem, or learning about a new way of tackling the problem, both of which help to expand my skills as a programmer.

A philosophy I live by is similar to the ‘no zero days’ philosophy. Even if life is tough, or hectic, or we’re just living in the moment, if each and every day we spend a little bit of time trying to improve ourselves, hopefully we’ll be a better person by the end of the day. This approach allows me to open myself up to new ideas, different methodologies for taking on various problems, and helping to create a supportive team environment for those around me.

Feel free to get in touch any time, and especially if you are an employer as I am currently looking for work.
